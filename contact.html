Our Focus Area

Wangui Gatere-Brenda's campaign focuses on all of Nyeri County, with special attention to the unique challenges facing women and marginalized communities in mountain regions.

Nyeri County covers 3,337 km² with 6 sub-counties and significant mountain regions including parts of the Aberdare Range.

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I volunteer for the campaign?

+

We welcome volunteers in various capacities including community mobilization, event coordination, social media support, and grassroots outreach. Please fill out the contact form indicating your interest in volunteering, and our volunteer coordinator will reach out to you with available opportunities.

Can Wangui visit our community or organization?

+

Yes! Wangui is committed to visiting communities across Nyeri County, especially in mountain regions and marginalized areas. Please use the contact form to send an invitation with details about your community, organization, and the purpose of the visit. We prioritize engagements that focus on women's empowerment, youth development, and community challenges.

How can I support the campaign financially?

+

Financial contributions help us reach more communities and implement our programs. For information on how to contribute, please contact us directly at info@wanguigatere.com. We maintain transparency and accountability in all campaign finances in accordance with Kenyan electoral laws.

What specific issues does Wangui address for mountain region communities?

+

Wangui focuses on unique challenges facing mountain communities including: limited infrastructure and connectivity, climate change impacts on agriculture, water access issues, healthcare accessibility in remote areas, and economic opportunities for women in these regions. Her vision includes specific programs tailored to these geographic challenges.

How can youth get involved in the campaign?

+

Youth are at the center of our campaign! Opportunities include: youth ambassador programs, digital content creation, community research and mapping, organizing youth forums, and participating in environmental initiatives. We also offer mentorship and leadership development for young people interested in public service.
