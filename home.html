Leadership Rooted in Service & Integrity

Wangui Gatere-Brenda is not just a political aspirant; she is a movement builder, community advocate, and visionary leader dedicated to transforming Nyeri County through principled, people-centered leadership.

With a background in administration from USIU University and years of grassroots community engagement, Wangui brings a unique combination of professional expertise and deep understanding of the challenges facing women, youth, and marginalized communities in Kenya's mountain regions.

  • USIU University Graduate – Academic excellence combined with practical leadership training
  • Administrative Specialist – Proven expertise in organizational management and community coordination
  • Grassroots Advocate – Deeply connected to the realities of Nyeri's mountain communities
  • Women & Youth Champion – Unwavering commitment to empowering vulnerable populations
